Athina Platform allows you to monitor your production LLM application, measure its performance and detect bad outputs.

It provides a seamless integration with your existing systems and enables you to evaluate the performance of your models against real-world production data.

## Why use Athina Monitoring

Athina's Monitoring Platform is designed for LLM developers and PMs.

- [Explore your usage and customer behavior](./analytics): Gain valuable insights into how your models are being used and understand user behavior patterns.

- [Measure your LLM performance](./performance): Measure and track the performance of your models, including response times and accuracy.

- [Detect Bad Outputs](./detect_bad_outputs): Detect and handle failures in real-time to ensure smooth and reliable model performance.

- [5 Minute Integration](../logging/openai_chat): Start logging your data in 5 mins, and we take care of the rest.
